How I work

Consultation

Whether it be face-to-face or over the phone, the first step is for me to get to know you and understand your business goals, needs and target audience.

Design

From our discussions, I’ll go away and produce some initial design concepts and create a wireframe of your new site. I’ll show you how it will look on desktop and on mobile. We can tweak the designs until you’re completely satisfied with how it will look.

Develop

Once the design concepts are approved, I’ll get to work building your site, making sure it’s fully responsive and renders properly across all browsers and devices.

Maintain

It’s important to keep the website up to date and the content current. I’ll help you maintain your site for as long as you require.

Responsive Design

With more and more people browsing the internet on mobile devices such as phones, tablets and desktop PCs (or laptops), it’s important for modern businesses to make sure their website is mobile friendly.

I build fully responsive websites that look great on a wide range of devices and adjust to any screen size.

Websites Designed and Built for EASE

I design websites that are easy to navigate for the end user and also easy for the client to update.
